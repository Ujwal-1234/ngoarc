ABOUT US

                        Aradhana Resource Centre Society is a Non Governmental Organization, registered under the Society Act in the Year 2008.

                            In the last 13 years The Organization has contributed its work towards upliftment of the poor in Health, Agriculture,

                            Economic & Education and Women Empowerment. 

                            Aradhana Resource Centre Society achieved them through Training Exposure, exhibition, Field visit and Awareness Programmes,

                            Also We Avail Credit Facilities To Self Help Groups(SHGs) by tie up with the Banks (namely Indian Bank, SBI, ICICI, etc..)
 
                            through Income Generation Programmes. For our Best Contribution Towards SHGs, We got A- GRADE CERTIFICATION From INDIAN BANK.

                        VISION & MISSION:

                            The Vision and Mission of the Aradhana Resource Centre Society is to Empower Women, Uplift the Poor & Marginalized People in Economy,

                             Health and Education. In Order to Achieve this Aradhana Resource Centre Society conducts Various helpful Events, Training, Programmes 

                             like Skill Development, Entrepreneurship, Awareness etc.. & works together with volunteers, Members in the field to Achieve those Objectives.


                        OBJECTIVES: 

                            ●	The Society has Chosen the Most Backward Villages in the Various Districts of Tamil Nadu. The Main focus is on liberating 

                                Individuals from Destructive Environment.

                            ●	To Ensure community development through Health Activities like Training, Awareness, Linking the government programmes 

                                with PHC & Private Hospital who gives treatment @ free of cost.

                            ●	Linkages with all government departments, Mobilization of all available Resources to fulfill the objectives of 

                                Health, Economic, Education & Empowerment of Women.

                        ACTIVITIES INITIATED:


                            ●	WELFARE - Education Support to Children, Free Tuition Centres, Rural Development Programmes, Vocational Training Institutes.

                            ●	Development - Fruit Seedling, Organic Farming, Family Counselling centre, Drugs & Smoking Rehabilitation Camps.

                            ●	CAMPAIGN -  Water Saving Campaign, Education, Forest Promotion.

                            ●	Women Empowerment -  Abort Eradication of Female Infanticide camps, Education to Females, Right to Equaity Camps.

                            ●	Providing Free Note Books To Poor.

                            ●	Provided free saplings To school and villages.



                        RESULTS ACHIEVED:


                            ●	40 Medical Camps.

                            ●	Promotion of Trees plantations in 25 schools and 116 Villages.

                            ●	Distributed “Law & Justice” protection book to 5000 women.

                            ●	Free Notebooks for poor children in 56 schools.

                            ●	More than 70 Awareness Programmes in more than 50 Villages.

                            ●	Taught Free Tuition for more than 200 Students.

                            ●	More than 350 women Benefited.
